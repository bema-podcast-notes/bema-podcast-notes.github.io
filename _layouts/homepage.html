What is BEMA?

The BEMA Podcast is a walk-through of the Bible and its surrounding historical context, hosted by Marty Solomon and co-hosted by Brent Billings. The podcast "deconstructs our common readings of the Bible and attempts to reconstruct them through the lens of historical context"‚Äîhelping listeners understand Scripture through its original ancient Hebrew and cultural setting rather than through modern Western assumptions.

The name "BEMA" comes from the Hebrew word meaning "platform" or "raised place"‚Äîhistorically, it's where the Torah was read in the synagogue. The podcast lives up to this name by carefully examining biblical texts, exploring what they meant to their original audiences, and discovering how that understanding transforms our reading today.

What You'll Find Here
